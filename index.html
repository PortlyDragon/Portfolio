ALBERT WHITE Portfolio

Everything in here is based on some games we made with my colleagues, this are my contributions to the games.

PLANETARIUM

Building randomizer

I developed a system that would take multiple pre created buildings and randomize them including the objects inside, every time the player press play they’ll find themselves in a different scenario, with different structures and challenges.

Rope Bridges

I managed to create a dynamic bridge in which both, players and enemies can interact with it’s physics, using tools to destroy parts of it or the whole thing when the game gets too intense.

Teleporting Enemy

To make things more difficult I game a type if enemy the power of teleport to the player’s location and attack, but if the player is able to move out of the enemy’s range, the enemy will go back to roaming mode before it’s teleportation cooldown resets.

EVIL ENCOUNTER

Speed bar

Speed was a big deal in this game, I added a bar that would fill up whenever the player reached high speed and depending on how full this bar was the weapon could use different types of bullets, butt by shooting or moving slow the speed bar would go down.

Main weapon

The main weapon of Evil Encounters is a double barrel gun, by pressing left click the gun fires a shotgun with the purpose of launching the player to gain more speed, and by right clicking the weapon becomes a DMR using the speed bar to do more damage to enemies.

shotgun Launch

The shotgun on the main weapon inflicts low damage to enemies, but by launching the player on the opposite direction they shoot the weapon, the speed bar fills giving the player more options oof attack.

DMR

The DMR weapon uses the speed bar to fire different types of bullets, each bullet is classified by color and each color inflicts different damage, for example if the bar is a 75% or higher the bullet will be purple inflicting the MAX damage.
